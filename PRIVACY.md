# Privacy Policy for App-Nag Blocker

**Last updated:** January 2026

## Data Collection

App-Nag Blocker does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information.

## What the Extension Stores

The extension stores only the following data locally on your device using Firefox's built-in storage API:

- **Enabled/disabled state**: A boolean indicating whether the extension is active
- **Whitelist**: A list of domain names you have chosen to whitelist (e.g., "example.com")

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

## Permissions Explained

- **storage**: Used to save your preferences (enabled state and whitelist) locally on your device
- **Content script on all URLs**: Required to detect and dismiss app-nag prompts on websites you visit. The extension only reads page content to identify prompts - it does not modify, collect, or transmit any page content or personal information.

## Third-Party Services

App-Nag Blocker does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

## Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in the extension update notes and this document.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at the project repository.
